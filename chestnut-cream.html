Creamy chestnut cream dessert

over 43K views

Creamy and tasty chestnut dessert, I promise you will not regret it.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 250g of mascarpone
  • 30g of white sugar
  • 500g of Chestnut cream
  • 40g of flour

Steps

  1. Separated the egg whites and yolks.
  2. Add the mascarpone
  3. Add the sugar and mix.
  4. Add the chestnut cream and mix.
  5. Beat the egg whites and don't forget to add a pinch of salt
  6. Add the egg whites in 3 steps
  7. 1/3 is added to the mixture and fermely mix.
  8. 1/3 is added again but now you need to mix it gently.
  9. Last part is added and it's were the magic happend so take your time and mix it gently
  10. Take your favorite cup and pour in it
  11. Now the hardest part. You have to let them reste for at least 5h. All night long will be prefered.
  12. And voila if you enjoy, let us know on tiktok and subscribe for more.
