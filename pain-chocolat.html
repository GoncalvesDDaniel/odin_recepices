Chocolat cake from Cyril Lignac

over 46K views

You will discover a soft and melty chocolat cake from a french cook Chef Cyril Lignac.

Ingredients

  • 250g of mascarpone
  • 4 eggs
  • 80g of white sugar
  • 200g of back chocolat
  • 40g of flour

Steps

  1. Put the mascarpone into a bowl, add one by one the eggs and mix between each one.
  2. Add the sugar and mix.
  3. Add the (melted) chocolat and mix.
  4. A this point you should probably pre heat the oven at 180°C
  5. Add the flour and ... mix again.
  6. Pour the mixture in a cake mold. You can additionnaly put some baking paper in it.
  7. Bake at 180°C for 25 min.
  8. And voila if you enjoy, let us know on tiktok and subscribe for more.
