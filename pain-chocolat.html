Easy Pain au chocolat

Quick and easy french pain au chocolat.

Ingredients

  • 2 Puff of pastery
  • 1 egg
  • milk
  • 200g of chocolat

Steps

  1. Put 1 puff on the top on the over one.
  2. Cut the corner to make the all thing square.
  3. Make equals rectangles.
  4. Put some chocolat along on side. Don't worry if you don't have bars of chocolat, the chocolat is goning to melt.
  5. Wrap all the chocolat you have put.
  6. Put another chocolat bar. And finish wraping you pain au chocolat.
  7. Now that you have made one, you should consider pre-head your over at 180°C.
  8. Mix the egg with some milk. And brush you backery with it.
  9. Bake at 180°C for 15 min.
